Q&A Page

  • Where can I find/buy Ukko’s safe flour?
    In any local grocery store!
  • Is it safe for people with allergies to eat Ukko safe flour? Has it been tested?
    Yes!, Ukko's safe flour is made out of 100% natural and healthy ingredients and is being tested on people with celiac disease to ensure that it is safe for everyone including non-celiacs.
  • Does it taste different from regular flour?
    Ukko's safe flour tastes exactly like regular flour and it has been apart of our mission to make sure that it resembles the taste of regular flour for everyone to enjoy!
  • What are the differences between regular flour and Ukko’s safe flour
    Ukko's safe flour is safe for everyone, wether you are gluten-free or not, Ukkos safe flour is the healthy alternative to regular flour and has multipule health benifts that arent in normal gluten free flour.
  • How do you create safe flour?
    We use a unique fermentation technique and advanced technology to create the flour and add all the health benifits needed to perfect Ukko's safe flour.
